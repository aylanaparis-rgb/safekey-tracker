SafeKey Tracking Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 20, 2026

Your privacy is our core software engineering mission. SafeKey handles data using an isolated architecture:

  • Zero Data Persistence: Geolocation coordinates are computed directly inside your browser cache layer. We do not transmit or store real addresses or GPS telemetry on our servers.
  • Anonymized Records: Registered payment keys match only an encrypted data string on our drive to protect identities.
  • No External Trackers: This framework operates entirely independent of third-party ad networks, telemetry scripts, or data-collection cookies.

By connecting to our portal dashboard, you grant permission to use local tracking protocols purely for real-time proximity math.
